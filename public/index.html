🔊 ECHO TAG

Total darkness. The only way to see anyone is to ping.

How to play
  • The arena is pitch black. You cannot see other players at all — until a sonar wave sweeps over them.
  • One hunter starts. Only hunters can fire a ping (Space / button) — an expanding wave that briefly lights up everyone it touches.
  • But pinging also reveals the hunter's own position to everyone. Hunt carefully.
  • Hunters tag hiders on contact — tagged hiders become hunters too.
  • Hiders win if anyone survives the timer. Hunters win if they catch everyone.
  • Move: WASD / arrows, or the joystick. The camera follows behind you.
